Name a Seat in the New Buttenwieser Hall

By naming a seat you are supporting the future of world-class performances at The 92nd Street Y, New York, investing in the artists of today and tomorrow, and helping this historic hall continue to inspire for generations to come.

As we complete a transformative renovation of The 92nd Street Y, New York’s Buttenwieser Hall before its reopening in spring, 2023, we invite you to become part of it all by naming a seat, furthering a legacy, and fueling the extraordinary creativity that is at the heart of this history-rich hall.

Buttenwieser Hall opened its doors on October 10, 1930 and was first used for religious services. In the more than 90 years since, it has been a treasured space for pioneering dance performances, boundary-breaking concerts, conversations with leading figures in the arts, film screenings, theater productions for young audiences, artist in residence explorations, dance classes, unparalleled workshops for dance educators, and more. We hope that your experiences in Buttenwieser Hall have been as varied and memorable as 92NY itself — and that you are ready to make new memories as we step forward into a completely transformed Buttenwieser Hall.

The renovationwill completely reimagine and reconfigure the hall, with a new retractable stage to maximize its multiple-use nature, telescopic seating, new state-of-the-art acoustics, cutting-edge recording and broadcast technology, new lighting and finishes, an open plan lobby, and more. Flooded with natural light, the new Buttenwieser Hall will become an inspiring space for today’s most exciting creators, and an inspired space for performances across the arts for generations to come.

Inscribing a seat plaque — for yourself or to honor a loved one or special occasion — will make you an indelible part of the creative innovation, groundbreaking performances, and incredible experiences to come as we begin the exciting next chapter of Buttenwieser Hall’s vibrant future.

The history of this hall is vast and deep. The future of this hall is ready to be written. We hope you'll be part of it.
